#1 Father #2 BSB #3 Darkbeast #4 Ludwig #5 Logarius #6 Amelia #7 Nurse #8 Gehrman #9 Laurence #10 Maria
#1 Father Gascoigne

Father Gascoigne is the first required boss and I feel hes the best teacher for beginners

#2 Blood-Starved Beast

Blood-Starved Beast is an early optional boss who becomes a good intermediate teacher

#3 Darkbeast Paarl

Darkbeast Paarl is a optional boss you can find pretty early on and a very difficult challenge

#4 Ludwig, The Holy Blade

Ludwig is one of my all time favorite bosses learning how to beat him was an amazing experience

#5 Martyr Logarius

Logarius can feel like an extremely unfair fight especially early on but with lots of practice he can be a very fun challenge

#6 Vicar Amelia

Amelia has an entire body for a hitbox making getting close scary at times but she is a good lesson in spacing

#7 Mergo’s Wet Nurse

The Nurse is one of the last bosses who cares for a baby “Great-one” names Mergo she can be very difficult fight at first but quick to learn

#8 Gehrman, the First Hunter

Gehrman is one of two possible final bosses and on the first experience with him a real pain but satisfying to get good against

#9 Laurence, the First Vicar

Laurence was a boss I had much trouble with but over time I learned not to step in lava

#10 Lady Maria

Lady Maria is one of the most challenging fights in the game but with a balanced move set and medium learning curve
